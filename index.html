FAQ

What is nightbio?

nightbio is a free link-in-bio tool. You get one short link (e.g. nightbio.lol/yourname) that leads to a page with your photo, bio, and all your links—socials, music, store, whatever you want.

Is it free?

Yes. You can create an account and build your bio page at no cost. We may offer optional paid features in the future.

How do I get my link?

After signing up, choose a username. Your link will be nightbio.lol/yourusername. You can share it anywhere—Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, etc.

Can I change my username or links later?

Yes. From your dashboard you can edit your profile, add or remove links, and update your bio anytime.

Who can see my profile?

Anyone with your link can view your public bio page. Only you can log in and edit your content.
